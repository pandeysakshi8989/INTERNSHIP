#### **1. Age Distribution Analysis**


#### **2. Category-Based Analysis**
- **Question:** Which product category generates the highest revenue?
- **Analysis:** Group by `Category` and sum `Purchase Amount (USD)`.

```python
category_revenue = shop.groupby('Category')['Purchase Amount (USD)'].sum().sort_values(ascending=False)
print(category_revenue)
category_revenue.plot(kind='bar', color='skyblue')
plt.title('Total Revenue by Product Category')
plt.xlabel('Category')
plt.ylabel('Total Revenue (USD)')
plt.show()
```

#### **3. Gender-Based Spending**
- **Question:** How does the purchase amount differ between genders?
- **Analysis:** Use a bar plot to compare average spending between males and females.

```python
gender_spending = shop.groupby('Gender')['Purchase Amount (USD)'].mean()
print(gender_spending)
sns.barplot(x=gender_spending.index, y=gender_spending.values)
plt.title('Average Purchase Amount by Gender')
plt.xlabel('Gender')
plt.ylabel('Average Purchase Amount (USD)')
plt.show()
```
